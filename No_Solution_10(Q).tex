\[
g(x) = \frac{f(x)}{x + 3}
\]  
and \(f(x)\) is a linear function.  

What is the \(y\)-intercept of the graph of \(y = f(x)\) in the \(xy\)-plane?

\[
\begin{array}{c|c}
x & g(x) \\
\hline
-15 & 3 \\
-6 & 0 \\
9 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\]

\choicebox{B}{(0, 4)}  
\choicebox{C}{(0, 8)}  
\choicebox{D}{(0, 24)}
